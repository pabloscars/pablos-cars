How I Get My Cars

Where they come from

Vehicles with cosmetic damage, hail damage, accident history, or theft-recovery history — mostly from salvage auctions and insurance channels.

What I look for

Solid mechanicals and a final price meaningfully below a clean-title version of the same car.

What I avoid

Cars needing major hidden mechanical work — frame damage repaired poorly, flood cars, anything where the story doesn't add up.

What you get

By the time it's listed, I know its story — and I'd rather tell you upfront than have you find it after you've paid.

Rule of thumb: if I wouldn't feel comfortable explaining a car's history to a friend, I don't buy it to resell.
